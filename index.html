About the department

Since 1990, the West Windsor Volunteer Fire Department has been serving the community of West Windsor as well as assisting the surrounding county.

Our cheif, assistant cheif, officers and volunteer force of 20+ respond to calls from the community year-round including brush fires, house fires, downed trees, car accidents, etc.
