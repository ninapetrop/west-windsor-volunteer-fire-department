Who and how we serve

The West Windsor Volunteer Fire Department serves West Windsor and sourounding towns, responding to fires, search and rescue, downed trees, vehicular accidents, and more.

Alarm and Strcuture Fires

Rapid response to building fires, prioritizing life safety and property protection.

Brush Fires

Combating wildfires in open areas, using specialized techniques and equipment.

Search and Rescue

Locating and extracting individuals trapped in hazardous situations.

First Response

Providing immediate medical assistance and emergency care at the scene.

Volunteers Needed!

Being a firefighter isn't just about battling flames--and many of our volunteers don't. Being a firefighter means supporting your community when need arises in whatever way you can. And the community of West Windosr needs your help.
