Burn permits

Permits MUST be obtained to have an open fire in Vermont. An open fire, no matter how small, should never be left unattended.

To request a burn permit, please contact Mike Spackman

  • 802-484-9142

Please plan ahead, as permit requests may take up to 24 hours to approve.
